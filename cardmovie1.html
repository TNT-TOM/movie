Directed by Joseph Kosinski (Top Gun: Maverick) and produced by Jerry Bruckheimer,
this fictional film was shot using real F1 cars and filmed during actual Grand Prix weekends.
Plot: The story follows Sonny Hayes (Brad Pitt), a retired Formula One driver from the 1990s,
who returns to the sport to mentor a hotshot rookie (Damson Idris) for a struggling fictional team called APXGP.
