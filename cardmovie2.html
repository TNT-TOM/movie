This action thriller is based on the 1981 novel of the same name by Robert Littell and was released by 20th Century Studios in April 2025. Plot: The film follows a brilliant but introverted CIA decoder named Charlie Heller (played by Rami Malek). After a London terrorist attack kills his wife, and his superiors refuse to intervene, Heller blackmails the agency into training him to hunt down those responsible for her death.
